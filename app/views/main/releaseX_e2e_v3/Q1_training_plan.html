Maths and English skills

Employers often say they want candidates with maths and English skills – and getting a qualification is one way to show you have those skills. Adult learning is taught in a relaxed and positive way, so there’s no need to be worried about going back into training.

Lots of different courses are taught in your area that offer flexible learning and part-time study to fit around your life. Many courses are free.

Tick all boxes that apply to you. You’ll get a personal action plan based on what you choose.
What will you learn on an English skills course?
On an adult learning English course you'll improve your confidence with reading and writing. Completing this course shows employers that you can communicate clearly at work in all kinds of situations.
What will you learn on a maths skills course?
On an adult learning maths course you'll improve your confidence with numbers. Completing this course shows employers that you can use maths at work.
