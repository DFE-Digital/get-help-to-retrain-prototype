Find training that boosts your job options

You could get a better job by adding to the skills you already have. Complete a short course in maths or English and you could find you have a wider range of job options. Many courses are free and offer flexible times to fit round your life.

This is a new scheme - a wider range of courses will be added in future.

You can also find on-the-job training that lets you earn while you learn.

What’s on offer?

Maths

Employers tell us they are looking for people who can show they have some maths skills. Completing a free maths course can really boost your job prospects in many different types of work.

More information on maths courses

English

Showing that you can communicate well at work is really important for your job prospects. English courses focus on giving you the skills you need to succeed in the workplace.

More information on English courses

On-the-job training

Gain skills and experience while continuing to earn. There’s no age limit and different levels of study are available. This means on-the-job-training is suitable for experienced older workers wanting to stay in work as they learn.

More help to change jobs

Get support to find and apply for jobs, or find other ways to move into a new role.
