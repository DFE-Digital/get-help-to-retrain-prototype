What will you learn on an English skills course?
On an adult learning English course you'll improve your confidence with reading and writing. Completing this course shows employers that you can communicate clearly at work in all kinds of situations.
What will you learn on an ESOL course?
ESOL courses are for people who don’t have English as their main language. You’ll improve your speaking, writing, listening and reading skills, which could make you more employable.
What will you learn on a maths skills course?
On an adult learning maths course you'll improve your confidence with numbers. Completing this course shows employers that you can use maths at work.
What will you learn on a computer skills course?
On a computer skills course you'll learn to confidently use a computer in the workplace. Completing this course shows employers you’re able to use a computer for a range of tasks at work.
