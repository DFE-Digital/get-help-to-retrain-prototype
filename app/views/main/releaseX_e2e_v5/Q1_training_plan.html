Building your action plan

Maths and English skills

It’s easier to find work and you may even earn more if you have maths and English qualifications. There’s no need to be worried about going back into training because:

  • courses are usually free
  • flexible, part time study fits round your life
  • adult learning classes are relaxed and positive

Select the courses you need. You’ll get a personal action plan based on what you choose.
What will you get from an English course?

Completing an adult learning English course shows employers that you can communicate clearly at work.

If English isn’t your main language, you can choose to do an English course aimed at speakers of other languages.

What will you get from a maths course?
Completing an adult learning maths course shows employers you are confident using numbers at work.

If none of these apply to you, just leave the boxes blank and select continue.
