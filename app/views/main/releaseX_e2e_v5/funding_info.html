Free training and help with training costs

You may be able to do free training or get money to help you train.

There’s lots of different funding support available. It’s usually best to talk to your course provider to find out what support you can get.

Free training

  • Many people can study maths and English for free.
  • You can study computer skills online for free on the Learn my Way website.

Other free courses are available. Speak to a local adviser to find out what’s available in your area. Call free on 0800 051 0459 Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm.

Help with training costs

  • You may be able to get funding to do courses that you need to pay for. This includes grants and bursaries, which you may not need to pay back.
  • If you’re facing financial hardship you could get extra help, including funding for your travel and accommodation costs, course materials and equipment, and childcare costs.

To find out more about funding support, find a course that suits your needs and then speak to the training provider to find out what funding you could get.
