Get help to retrain

Use this service to find out what you need to do to retrain for another job.

You’ll get the most out of this service if both of the following apply to you:

  • you don’t have a degree
  • you’re based in the Greater Manchester area
Start now

Before you start

To get advice and support while you use this service, you’ll need to provide:

  • your postcode
  • your date of birth
  • your email

What happens next

After you’ve used this service, you’ll be able to do the following:

  • retrain and search for jobs to apply to
  • take part in research by email, but you can opt out
