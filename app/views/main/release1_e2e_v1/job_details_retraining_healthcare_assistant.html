Retrain to be a healthcare assistant

Provide high-quality and compassionate health and social care for a wide range of people

Salary

  • £10,000 – £17,500

Typical hours

35-40 a week

You could work

Monday to friday

What you’ll do as a healthcare assistant

  • helping patients shower and get dressed
  • helping people eat
  • making beds
  • using equipment to lift and move patients
  • talking to patients and reassuring them
  • helping patients to the toilet
  • tidying the ward or patients’ homes
  • taking patients' temperature or pulse
  • attending meetings with other healthcare professionals

You'll need:

  • patience and the ability to remain calm in stressful situations
  • sensitivity and understanding
  • to be thorough and pay attention to detail
  • the ability to work well with others
  • the ability to accept criticism and work well under pressure
  • the ability to work well with your hands
  • excellent verbal communication skills
  • customer service skills
