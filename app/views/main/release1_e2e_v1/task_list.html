Get help to retrain

Check what you need to do to retrain for another job. You'll get the most out of this service if do not have a degree.

How long it takes you to complete the following steps will depend on your situation. You will be able to save your progress and come back to this service at another time if you need to.

  1. 1. Check what you are good at

    • Check your existing skills 5 minutes

  2. 2. Explore the type of jobs you could do

    • Search for the type of jobs you could retrain to do 5 to 20 minutes

  3. 3. Apply for a training course

    • Find and apply to training courses near you 10 to 20 minutes

  4. 4. What to do after your training course

    • Check what you can do after you finish your training course
