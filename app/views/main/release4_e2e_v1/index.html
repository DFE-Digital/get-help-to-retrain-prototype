Get help to retrain

Use this service to find out what other jobs you can do with your current skills. You can also find out how to get new skills that could help you find a better job.

This service is designed to help if:

  • you're employed
  • you don't have a degree
  • you're aged 24 or over
  • and you're earning below £35,000 a year

If these don’t all apply to you, try visiting the National Careers Service or your local Jobcentre Plus instead.
