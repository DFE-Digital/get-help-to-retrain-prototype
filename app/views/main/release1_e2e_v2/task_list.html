Get help to retrain

Check what you need to do to retrain for another job. How long it takes you to complete the following steps will depend on your situation.

  1. 1. Check what you are good at

    • Check your existing skills 5 minutes

      You’ll be surprised at what you already know

  2. 2. Explore the types of jobs you could do

    • Search for the types of jobs you could retrain to do 5 to 20 minutes

      These are not live jobs you can apply to – they’re the types of jobs you’ll be able to do with your existing skills or once you do some training

  3. 3. Apply for a training course

    • Talk to an adviser to find and apply to a training course near you 10 to 20 minutes

      Training can help you access a broader range of jobs

  4. 4. Find out what you can do next

    • Get more support to help you on your new career path

      For example, advice on how to look and apply for jobs or help with childcare options

  5. 5. Help improve this service

    • Take a quick survey and tell us about your experience 3 to 5 minutes

      This is a new service – your feedback will help us improve it
