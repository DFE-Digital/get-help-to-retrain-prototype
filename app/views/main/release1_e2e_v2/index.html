Get help to retrain

Use this service to discover your skills, explore jobs you could do and check training courses that will help you find a better job.

Why you need to retrain

Even if you’re already employed, some training – for example, a free short course in maths or English – will improve your chances of getting a more stable, higher paid job, and it means you’ll be less likely to lose your job in the future.

You’ll get personalised advice and support along the way, so you can find training that fits around your schedule and personal needs – for example, with childcare or IT skills, or if you have not studied in a while and feel unsure.
Start now

Before you start

You’ll get the most out of this service if all of the following apply to you:

  • you're employed
  • you do not have a degree
  • you’re based in the Liverpool area
  • you're aged 24 or over
