Find and apply to a training course near you

Call the Get help to retrain helpline to speak with an adviser and get free guidance. They'll be able to recommend training courses near you.

Why you need to retrain

Even if you’re already employed, some training – for example, a free short course in maths or English – will improve your chances of accessing a broader range of jobs.

You’ll get personalised advice and support along the way, so you can find training that fits around your schedule and your personal needs – for example, with childcare or IT skills, or if you have not studied in a while and feel unsure about what you should do.
